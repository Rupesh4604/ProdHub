Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

👋

1. Overview

ProdHub is a personal productivity application that helps you manage tasks, habits, notes, and goals. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being fully transparent about how we handle your data.

📋

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account data – Your Google account name, email address, and profile picture, used for authentication via Firebase Authentication.
  • App data – Tasks, habits, notes, goals, and related entries stored in Cloud Firestore under your unique user ID.
  • Google Calendar data – If you sync your calendar, we request read-only access to display events. We never store calendar data on our servers.
⚙️

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and display your personal data.
  • To sync your productivity data across devices.
  • To display your Google Calendar events within the schedule view.
🔒

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication), protected by Google's enterprise-grade security. Your data is accessible only to your authenticated account. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

🔗

5. Third-Party Services

ProdHub uses the following Google services, governed by Google's Privacy Policy:

  • Firebase Authentication – identity management
  • Cloud Firestore – data storage
  • Firebase Hosting – app delivery
  • Google Calendar API – read-only event display (optional)
✅

6. Your Rights

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app.
  • Revoke Google Calendar access at any time via your Google Account settings.
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us via GitHub.
🧒

7. Children's Privacy

ProdHub is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

📬

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
